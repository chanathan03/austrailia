Tips & Reminders

⚡

Book Everything Early

Dec 20–Jan 1 is peak Australian summer holiday season. Hotels, reef tours, and restaurants fill up months in advance. Start booking NOW — especially Sydney NYE (harbourside hotels/restaurants book out 6 months ahead!) and Christmas Day dinner in Cairns.

☀️

Sun Safety is Critical

  • Australia has the world's highest UV levels
  • Apply SPF 50+ every 2 hours
  • 11am–3pm is peak UV — seek shade
  • Kids need hats AND rashguards on beaches
  • Sunscreen is expensive in Australia — bring extra!
🦟

Cairns & Bugs

  • Cairns is tropical — bring strong DEET repellent
  • Daintree has mosquitoes and sandflies
  • Cover up at dusk especially
  • Check for Ross River virus precautions
💳

Money & Payments

  • Australia is almost fully cashless — cards work everywhere
  • Notify your bank before travel
  • Get a Wise or Charles Schwab card for no-fee ATM withdrawals
  • Tipping is NOT mandatory in Australia (optional, ~10%)
🚌

Getting Around

  • Brisbane: Get a go card for buses, trains & ferries
  • Sydney: Get an Opal card for trains/buses/ferries
  • The free 50c CityHopper ferry in Brisbane is a great river cruise!
  • Uber works well in all 3 cities
  • Airtrain from Brisbane Airport to CBD ~$20 AUD
🐊

Wildlife Safety

  • Don't swim in estuaries/rivers in Cairns — saltwater crocs!
  • Only swim at patrolled beaches (look for flags)
  • Check for jellyfish advisories — stingers season is Oct–May in QLD
  • Wear stinger suits when snorkeling — tour provides these
🎄

Christmas & New Year

  • Christmas in Australia = summer! Beach BBQs are the norm
  • Many shops closed Dec 25 & Jan 1 — stock up beforehand
  • Sydney NYE fireworks over the Harbour Bridge = world's best!
  • Family fireworks at 9 PM in Sydney — great for younger kids
  • Book NYE harbourside dinner/viewing 3–6 months ahead
📱

Useful Apps

  • Translink — Brisbane public transport
  • Opal Travel — Sydney public transport
  • Google Maps — works great in all 3 cities
  • Uber — rideshare across Australia
  • Zomato / Google — restaurant bookings
🧳

Flying Between Cities

  • Arrive 90 min before domestic flights (security is quick)
  • Qantas has the best family amenities on domestic routes
  • Jetstar is cheaper but check baggage fees — can add up!
  • All 3 airports have family lanes at security
🌊

Great Barrier Reef Tips

  • Book morning tours — afternoon seas get choppier
  • Take motion sickness meds the night before if susceptible
  • Underwater cameras/GoPros are worth renting on the boat
  • Use reef-safe sunscreen ONLY — protect the coral!
